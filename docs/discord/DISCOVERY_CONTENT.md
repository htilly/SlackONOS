## Submission Notes

### What Makes SlackONOS Unique?

1. **True Democracy:** Unlike other music bots that use command-based voting, SlackONOS uses intuitive emoji reactions for seamless voting.

2. **Gong Ban System:** Tracks that get "gonged" (voted down) become temporarily immune to re-queuing, preventing trolling.

3. **Multi-Platform:** First bot to support both Discord and Slack simultaneously on the same Sonos system.

4. **Self-Hosted:** No data leaves your server. Perfect for privacy-conscious organizations.

5. **AI Integration:** Natural language processing makes adding music feel like talking to a DJ, not a computer.

### Target Audience

- **Gaming Communities:** Discord servers that want shared music during gaming sessions
- **Remote Teams:** Companies using Discord for team communication
- **Co-working Spaces:** Shared offices with Sonos speakers
- **Streamers:** Content creators who want community-controlled music
- **Music Enthusiasts:** Any group that wants fair, democratic music selection

### Competitive Advantages

vs. **Rythm/Groovy (RIP):**
- Controls physical Sonos speakers, not just bot voice channels
- Democratic voting system built-in
- Self-hosted (won't get shut down by Discord/Google)

vs. **FredBoat/MEE6:**
- Actual hardware integration (Sonos speakers)
- Community voting determines play order
- AI natural language understanding
- Multi-platform support (Discord + Slack)

vs. **Sonos Official App:**
- Accessible from Discord (no app switching)
- Democratic control (not just one person's phone)
- Vote-based queue management
- Command history and transparency
